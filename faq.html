The duration of results for our cosmetic treatments, including HydraFacial, dermal fillers, and Botox, can vary. HydraFacial results may be noticeable immediately and can last for several weeks. Dermal fillers typically provide results that can last anywhere from six months to two years, depending on the type of filler used. Botox results are temporary and may last three to six months.
It's important to note that individual responses to treatments can vary, and factors such as skin type, lifestyle, and treatment specifics can influence the duration of results. During your consultation, our skilled practitioners will discuss the expected longevity of each treatment, ensuring you have a clear understanding of what to expect and can make informed decisions based on your aesthetic goals.
The downtime after our cosmetic treatments varies depending on the specific procedure. For HydraFacial, there is typically little to no downtime, and you can resume your daily activities immediately. Dermal filler treatments may involve some swelling or bruising, with downtime ranging from a few hours to a few days. Botox injections usually have minimal downtime, and most individuals can return to their regular routines right away.
It's important to note that individual responses to treatments can vary, and our practitioners will provide personalized post-treatment care instructions during your consultation. These guidelines will help minimize potential downtime and ensure a comfortable and smooth recovery process tailored to your needs.

Dermal filler treatments may involve some mild discomfort, typically alleviated with the use of a topical anesthetic or numbing cream. Botox injections are generally well-tolerated, causing only minor, brief discomfort.
